B12 Marathon is a premier organization dedicated to promoting health and fitness through marathon events. With a passion for fostering community engagement, we specialize in organizing impactful races that inspire participants to achieve their fitness goals. Our team is committed to delivering unforgettable experiences, combining the thrill of running with the joy of supporting charitable causes. Join us in our mission to make a difference through the power of running.

Objectives

Our main objective is to organize marathon events that promote physical fitness and contribute to charitable causes. We aim to help the less fortunate in our community through the funds raised from our events. By combining fitness with philanthropy, we strive to make a positive impact on society while inspiring individuals to lead healthier lifestyles.

Technologies

We specialize in the development of Spring Boot Java projects to enhance the efficiency and performance of our marathon management systems. Our cutting-edge technologies enable us to deliver seamless event experiences for participants and organizers alike.
